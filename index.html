Jing Ke(柯晶)

PI, Computational Kernel Lab



                    
Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/ citations?user=zX41yC8AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN


简介：博士毕业于澳大利亚新南威尔士大学，现任上海交通大学计算机系副教授、博导，并任新南威尔士大学联席高级讲师（Adjunct Senior Lecturer）。目前主要的研究方向为 GPU 体系结构与高性能并行计算，涵盖算子库与编译优化、内核调度与内存层次加速，并在病理图像高通量诊断等应用场景开展相关研究。在 S3 Graphics，AMD 和 CSIRO 等机构有多年的商业经验，与著名国产GPU算力公司长期保持联合研究与产学研合作。围绕算子优化、张量并行、模型量化、异构计算等方向，构建硬件、软件、应用的全栈AI。相关工作发表于 IEEE TMI、Medical Image Analysis，IPDPS、MICRO、DAC 等期刊会议，并在并行计算社区开源若干工具与基准。出版著作“通用图形处理器设计-GPGPU编程模型与架构原理”（清华大学出版社）。主持国家自然科学基金青年项目，上海市自然科学基金面上项目及产学研重大研究项目。

课题组常年欢迎积极主动、执行力强的本科生、硕士生和博士生加入，尤其欢迎对 GPU 体系结构、高性能并行计算 以及与国产 GPU 企业联合研发、工程落地与产学研对接感兴趣的同学联系交流。

Jing Ke is currently an associate professor in the Computer Science Department at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. She graduated from University of New South Wales as a Ph.D. She has been conferred the academic title of Adjunct Senior Lecturer by the University of New South Wales. Jing’s main academic focus is in GPU architecture and high-performance parallel computing, covering operator-library and compiler optimization, kernel scheduling, and memory-hierarchy acceleration. She also conducts related research in application scenarios such as high-throughput pathology image analysis. She has more than six years of industrial experience at AMD (Advanced Micro Devices) and VIA Technologies, the world's leading GPU graphics processor research and development companies. She also maintains long-term joint research and industry collaborations with leading domestic GPU computing companies. Her work spans operator optimization, tensor parallelism, model quantization, and heterogeneous computing, with an emphasis on building full-stack AI systems across hardware, software, and applications. She has also published over ten conference papers in architecture and parallel computing, e.g., MICRO, DAC and IPDPS, as well as a textbook on GPU architecture analysis. She has been awarded an NSFC Young Scientists Fund, a Shanghai Natural Science Foundation General Program grant, and several other fundings from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Our lab welcomes highly motivated and action-oriented students at all levels (Bachelor's, Master's, Ph.D.) to join us. We strongly value candidates interested in GPU architecture and parallel computing, particularly those eager to engage in joint R&D with domestic GPU manufacturers to bridge the gap between academic research and engineering implementation.
